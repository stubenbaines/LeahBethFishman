ABOUTLEAH BETH

Leah Beth Fishman is a New York based artist specializing in gilding, the art of gold leaf application and finishes. She has been trained by master gilders of world reknown in traditional techniques of gilding, gilding restoration and other finishes. Experienced in working on a large and small scale, past projects have ranged from the (ongoing) restoration of St. Alphonsus' Church in New Orleans (featured in an episode of the HBO series 'Treme') to gilded interiors in Trump Plaza, several lamps made custom for the Oval Office redecoration of 2010 and chandeliers & glass panels for Tiffany & Co. showrooms around the world. She is accomplished in gilded signage and window/ glass work including many businesses and offices in Rockefeller Plaza and throughout the New York City area. She creates original gilded artwork as well as custom/ personalized works for such as murals, paintings, hand made and painted mirrors, carved and gilded inlaid lettering, decorative glassware and guitar and motorcycle detailing.

Works can be commissioned or personalized in any number of ways. These can include:

  • hand made/ painted and personalized mirrors
  • large murals/ panels
  • decorative glassware (monograms, wedding commemoration, etc)
  • motorcycle and other automotive detailing
  • guitar detailing
  • architectural detailing
